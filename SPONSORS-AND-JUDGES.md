# Sponsors, Judges & Program Staff

[← Back to main archive](README.md)

Institutional context for the Stella Zhang New Venture Competition: financial backers, the naming gift, judging panels by year (where named publicly), and program staff. Last updated 2026-05-11.

## Naming Gift

### Initial gift (January 2022)

- **Donors:** Stella Zhang (FEMBA '05) and her husband John Shen
- **Term:** Five-year naming commitment (2021 to 2026)
- **Amount:** Not publicly disclosed at the time
- **Bridge to the competition:** Zhang learned of the NVC through Zarina Bahadur (MIE '20), the 2020 Grand Prize winner with Baby Box, who joined Zhang's Long Beach Accelerator portfolio
- **Zhang's stated motivation:** "The success of a business should not be solely measured by profits. A business should support and give back to the community wherever it can."

Source: [Merage News (Jan 2022)](https://merage.uci.edu/news/2022/01/New-Venture-Competition-Receives-Naming-Gift-from-Stella-Zhang-FEMBA-05.html)

### Second gift (March 2025)

- **Amount: $750,000**, extending the naming commitment for an additional five years (2026 to 2031)
- This is the gift that raised the announced annual prize pool from $100,000 to over $150,000, visible in the 2025 archive entry ("Total Prize Pool: Over $150,000 (Cash & In-Kind)")

Source: [Merage Press Release (Mar 2025)](https://merage.uci.edu/press-releases/2025/03/Stella-Zhang-Gives-750K-to-UC-Irvine-New-Venture-Competition.html)

### Stella Zhang background

- FEMBA '05 (Fully-Employed MBA, Paul Merage School of Business, UCI)
- Founder / COO, American Lending Center
- Co-founder, Long Beach Accelerator
- Delivered the UCI Merage commencement address, June 2024 ([source](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20240618913878/en/))

## Mentor / Coach Structure

The NVC has no standalone "named" mentor program. Finalists are paired with:

- **Industry mentors** through the Beall Center
- **SCORE mentors** (the SBA-affiliated volunteer business advisory program)

The coach / judge role overlaps in practice. Named individuals like Dan Jenkins, David Ochi, and Larry Wilk function as both.

## Competition Format

The NVC runs a structured **seven-month workshop series** leading up to the Grand Finale, with sessions such as:

- "How to Come up with a Billion Dollar Idea"
- "From Idea to Business"
- "Is there a Market for Your Product?"

Source: [Merage workshop event archive (Jan 2022)](https://merage.uci.edu/events/2022/01/nvc-workshop-012422.html)
