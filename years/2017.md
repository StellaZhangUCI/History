## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$64,500. Standard track payouts were $4,000 (1st) / $1,500 (2nd) this year, considerably smaller than the $10,000 / $5,000 standard adopted from 2018 onward.
- The competition included a Poverty Alleviation track (1st only) and a School-of-Medicine Grand Prize that year; both categories disappear in later editions.
- Donald "Don" Magnuson appeared on two finalist teams in 2017: Purist (Grand Prize SoM, Tech Surge 1st) and COMPAS (Business Products 2nd).
