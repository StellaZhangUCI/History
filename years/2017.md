## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$85,500 (NVC core tracks $58,000 + Tech Surge honorable mentions $5,000 + Outstanding Undergraduate $1,000 + co-located Designing Solutions for Poverty Alleviation pool $20,000 + $7,500 APIC + others). Standard track payouts were $4,000 (1st) / $1,500 (2nd) this year, considerably smaller than the $10,000 / $5,000 standard adopted from 2018 onward.
- The competition slate included a co-located **Designing Solutions for Poverty Alleviation** competition hosted by UCI's Blum Center (1st + two runners-up) and a School-of-Medicine Grand Prize; both categories disappear in later NVC editions.
- Primary source: [Merage 2017 NVC press release](https://merage.uci.edu/press-releases/2017/05/fifteen-teams-walk-away-with-more-than-100,000-in-cash-and-prizes-following-final-round-of-uci-new-venture-competition.html).
- Donald "Don" Magnuson appeared on two finalist teams in 2017: Purist (Grand Prize SoM, Tech Surge 1st) and COMPAS (Business Products 2nd).

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Purist** (Grand Prize, SoM): OPERATING. Founder Leila Safavi runs `puristinc.com`; ORNL Innovation Crossroads fellow; received $223K NIH SBIR (2019).
- **APIC Diagnostics** (Tech Surge 2nd): No public outcome found.
- **Esqalate** (Poverty Alleviation 1st): Likely DORMANT.
- **BrainCheck** (Consumer Products 1st): Likely never commercialized; was a UCI senior design / research project.
- **COMPAS** (Business Products 2nd): PIVOTED. IP licensed in 2017 to OpsGuru, a commercial spin-out (CEO Jeff Greenberg) that has no public footprint after 2018. Underlying technology continued internally inside UCI Health. (See note below on team-roster correction.)
- **Venom-Aid, Salux Diagnostics, Failsafe, Memorlane, Eater Zone, Element: B2B2C**: All track winners researched on 2026-05-12; no public outcomes found post-2017.

See [`OUTCOMES.md`](../OUTCOMES.md#2017) for the full breakdown and sources.
