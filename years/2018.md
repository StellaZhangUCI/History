## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$92,500. No overall Grand Prize / Runner-Up was awarded that year; track-award winners were the top-billed teams.
- Daniel Dai (Graduate Data) returned in 2018 after winning the 2017 Grand Prize (School of Medicine) with Purist.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Mechanodontics** (Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING. Rebranded as **BRIUS Technologies** (Carrollton, TX); rebrand confirmed via Crunchbase / CB Insights. Commercial launch of the Brava hidden-braces system on 2022-06-30. Total funding figures (~$46M) reported via PitchBook but not independently verified here.
- **Wing** (Consumer Services 1st): OPERATING. Rebranded as **Wing Assistant**; raised a $2.1M seed in October 2021; still operating as a managed-VA service in 2025/26.
- **CarDana** (Social Enterprise 2nd): OPERATING. Transparent charitable vehicle-donation platform; `cardana.co` live with active KBB-based donation flow and a 20% flat-fee model.
- **Closed Loop Plastics** (Tech Surge 2nd): Likely SHUT DOWN. Website now serves a Sedo parking page; last press coverage 2021.
- **First Step Diagnostics** (Life Sciences 2nd + Tech Surge 1st): No public outcome found. An earlier draft linked them to NeuroQure; verification on 2026-05-11 disconfirmed that connection.
- **Apeel Juice** (Social Enterprise 1st): No public outcome found.
- **ARTEM** (Consumer Products 1st): No public outcome found.
