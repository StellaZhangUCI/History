## Notes

- **Disclosed cash total:** $61,000 per the official NVC-2021-Report.pdf primary source ("$61,000 in CASH awarded to teams"). Several individual track-award amounts are still undisclosed in indexed press materials (Health Libero, MONI 1st, Hydroflow, HydroVasc).
- Project descriptions for every entry are now quoted from the official 2021 NVC Summary report.

## By the numbers (per 2021 NVC Summary)

- 85 Concept Paper submissions
- 54 Semi-Finalists in the Boardroom Round
- 10 Finalists
- ~300 participants
- 39 coaches
- 59 judges

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Shaka** (Grand Prize): OPERATING. Now **Shaka Culture Inc.** (Kansas City, MO). Raised ~$120K (Techstars, InnoVenture Iowa, ISA Ventures, UCI Merage). Microsoft Teams certified.
- **StyloSonic** (Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING (research and early stage). PitchBook profile current. 2022 peer-reviewed paper in *Photoacoustics*.
- **REMSYS** (Consumer Services 1st, tied): OPERATING (BETA). `remsys.app` in active BETA development as REMSYS LLC. Offers AI resume tailoring, cover-letter generation and mock-interview features.
- **Circular** (Social Enterprise 1st): Likely defunct. Was raising $400K for a fall 2022 pilot. No follow-on activity found.
