## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$41,000. Corrected 2026-05-12 from $46,000 after the Merage 2021-06 primary source clarified that MoodCloud and REMSYS tied for Consumer Services 1st and **split a single $5,000 prize** ($2,500 each), rather than each receiving $5,000 as previously recorded. Shaka's $17,500 total (Grand Prize + Business Products 1st combined) was confirmed via Merage source on 2026-05-11. Remaining track-award amounts undisclosed: Health Libero, MONI, Hydroflow, HydroVasc.
- 2021 ran during the COVID-era format and emphasized remote-collaboration projects.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Shaka** (Grand Prize): OPERATING. Now **Shaka Culture Inc.** (Kansas City, MO); raised ~$120K (Techstars, InnoVenture Iowa, ISA Ventures, UCI Merage); Microsoft Teams certified.
- **StyloSonic** (Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING (research/early stage). PitchBook profile current; 2022 peer-reviewed paper in *Photoacoustics*.
- **REMSYS** (Consumer Services 1st, tied): OPERATING (BETA). `remsys.app` in active BETA development as REMSYS LLC; offers AI resume tailoring, cover-letter generation, and mock-interview features.
- **Circular** (Social Enterprise 1st): Likely defunct. Was raising $400K for a fall 2022 pilot; no follow-on activity found.
