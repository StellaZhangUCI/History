## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$100,000.
- Dylan Riffle (Two Cube, Life Sciences 1st) returned in 2024 after appearing on Leprendo in 2023.
- Three 2024 NVC teams also won sibling UCI competitions in 2024 (per the Beall and Butterworth Competition 2024 winners page): **Sentinel** (NVC Grand Prize + BP 1st) won the **Butterworth Product Development Competition 1st Place** ($10,000) and additionally placed **2nd at the C&C Innovative Solutions SoCal Super Pitch Competition** ($1,250) per UCI ANTrepreneur Center 2024-09 coverage; **Two Cube** (NVC Life Sciences 1st) won **Butterworth 2nd Place**; **NeoMold** (NVC Runner-Up + LS 2nd) won the **Beall Student Design Competition 1st Place**. The same primary sources use slightly different spellings for two Sentinel members ("Nathan Ampudia" + "Spike O'Carroll") than the Business Wire / Merage 2024 press release ("Natan Ampudia" + "Spike O'Carrol"); the archive preserves the Business Wire / Merage spellings as the more authoritative. The ANTrepreneur 2024-09 profile names a "Redford Hudson (Data Science)" as part of the post-NVC Sentinel team, not yet confirmed to have been on the competing roster.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Sentinel** (Grand Prize + Business Products 1st): OPERATING (likely, MVP stage as of Sept 2024). A research-agent claim that Sentinel was absorbed by Spotter.ai's "Sentinel" platform was inferred from product-name overlap and is **unverified** on 2026-05-11; neither the UCI source nor the Spotter.ai page documents a connection.
- **Quiver** (Consumer Services 1st): NO public outcome found (reclassified 2026-05-12 after brand-disambiguation). An earlier draft mapped the 2024 NVC Quiver to thequiver.com (surfboard-rental marketplace est. 2015 by Joe Wilson and Rob Bonvetti); that connection is disconfirmed — thequiver.com is a separate commercial entity predating the 2024 NVC by 9 years.
- **DermaVision** (Social Enterprise 2nd): OPERATING (likely, student stage). **DermaVision Technologies'** multispectral bruise-imaging tool competed in the 2024 BMES Medtronic Student Design Competition (sources conflict on placement: BMES official says First Place; team's own site says third). UC Tech Transfer NCD/34412 currently says "not available for licensing" as of 2026-05-11.
- **NeoMold** (Runner-Up + Life Sciences 2nd): OPERATING (very early stage). `neomold.uci.design` up; pre-incorporation/design-review.
- **Crafty Palate** (Consumer Services 2nd): DORMANT (likely). Surfaces only as an early-stage signup form; no operating storefront.
- **Two Cube** (Life Sciences 1st): No public outcome found.
