## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$70,000. ELF Therapeutics' Tech Surge prize was reported as "Funded" rather than a fixed cash amount; Curbd / SwappIt tied with no public prize amount; Steth 'n Go's amount wasn't published.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **ELF Therapeutics** (Tech Surge 1st + Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING. Now branded **eLysis Inc.** Raised $400K+ non-dilutive; completed pig animal study; working toward FDA 510(k).
- **VolunteerCrowd** (Social Enterprise 1st): PIVOTED, then likely SHUT DOWN. Discontinued membership/verification/app on Jan 1, 2023; `volunteercrowd.com` now returns HTTP 404.
- **Steth 'n Go** (Consumer Products 2nd): OPERATING. Magnetic stethoscope holder still selling at `stethngo.com` with bulk pricing for healthcare facilities.
- **Poo Pal** (Consumer Products 1st): DORMANT (likely). Product listings remain on Amazon / Etsy but no recent company-side activity.

See [`OUTCOMES.md`](../OUTCOMES.md#2019) for the full breakdown and sources.
