## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$70,000. ELF Therapeutics' Tech Surge prize was reported as "Funded" rather than a fixed cash amount; Curbd / SwappIt tied and each received $5,000 per the Orange County Startups Council coverage; Steth 'n Go's amount wasn't published.
- **Cross-competition recognition (added 2026-05-12 per UCI Beall and Butterworth 2019 winners page)**: Two 2019 NVC teams also competed at sibling UCI competitions in the same year: **giv** (NVC SE 2nd) won the **Butterworth Software Competition 2019 2nd Place** ($6,500); **UniSafe** (NVC BP 1st) was a Finalist at the same Butterworth Software Competition. The bbcomp source also disconfirms a prior BACKLOG speculation: the 2019 NVC "giv" team is NOT the same as the Givsum platform (Shawn Wehan / Robert Kollar; joined Wayfinder 2017) - they are separate entities.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **ELF Therapeutics** (Tech Surge 1st + Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING. Now branded **eLysis Inc.** Raised $400K+ non-dilutive; completed pig animal study; working toward FDA 510(k).
- **VolunteerCrowd** (Social Enterprise 1st): PIVOTED, then likely SHUT DOWN. Discontinued membership/verification/app on Jan 1, 2023; `volunteercrowd.com` now returns HTTP 404.
- **Steth 'n Go** (Consumer Products 2nd): OPERATING. Magnetic stethoscope holder still selling at `stethngo.com` with bulk pricing for healthcare facilities.
- **Poo Pal** (Consumer Products 1st): DORMANT (likely). Product listings remain on Amazon / Etsy but no recent company-side activity.
- **myHealth Today** (Consumer Services 1st): OPERATING (likely). Kevin Wu (MBA '20) led the team; company officially incorporated June 2020 with UCI Beall Applied Innovation funding. Post-2020 status not independently verified on 2026-05-12 (site myhealthtoday.care returns ECONNREFUSED).
- **Embryologic** (Life Sciences 2nd): No public outcome found; the team (Prof. Michelle Digman lab) also placed 2nd in the 2019 Rice Business Plan Competition semifinal round.
