## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$100,000.
- This was the first edition under the *Stella Zhang New Venture Competition* name, following Stella Zhang's (FEMBA '05) naming gift announced in January 2022.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Noveil** (Grand Prize): OPERATING (low recent activity). Funded; Arch Grants recipient (St. Louis-based after relocating); Tracxn shows still active in early 2026 but ranked 750th of 1,851 dating apps.
- **Blue Aqua Food Tech** (Social Enterprise 1st): OPERATING. `bafoodtech.com` live; black soldier fly farm running; pre-revenue/early.
- **Enjovu Paper** (Runner-Up): No public outcome found.
- **forMED Technologies** (Life Sciences 1st): No public outcome found.
