## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$100,000.
- This was the first edition under the *Stella Zhang New Venture Competition* name, following Stella Zhang's (FEMBA '05) naming gift announced in January 2022.
- **Cross-competition recognition (added 2026-05-12 per UCI Beall and Butterworth past-winners pages)**: Two 2022 NVC teams had also competed in sibling UCI competitions a year earlier: **forMED Technologies** (NVC LS 1st) won the **Beall Student Design Competition 2021 1st Place** with the same intraocular-pressure project; **Nutripair** (NVC BP 1st) placed **2nd in the Butterworth Product Development Competition 2021** the year before the NVC win.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Noveil** (Grand Prize): OPERATING (low recent activity). Funded; Arch Grants recipient (St. Louis-based after relocating); Tracxn shows still active in early 2026 but ranked 750th of 1,851 dating apps.
- **Sayenza Biosciences** (Life Sciences 2nd): OPERATING. Clinical-stage adipose-tissue regenerative-medicine company. Raised ~$1.16M; joined California Life Sciences FAST Spring 2024 cohort.
- **Nutripair** (Business Products 1st): OPERATING. Founder Catlin Tran's app received a $25K Verizon grant in 2025 and was profiled by UCI Social Sciences in March 2025.
- **Blue Aqua Food Tech** (Social Enterprise 1st): OPERATING. `bafoodtech.com` live; black soldier fly farm running; pre-revenue/early.
- **SnapHealth** (Consumer Services 2nd): PIVOTED (likely). `mysnaphealth.com` operating with a new "health coverage" platform; differs from the 2022 NVC concept of patient health-record control.
- **Enjovu Paper** (Runner-Up): No public outcome found.
- **forMED Technologies** (Life Sciences 1st): No public outcome found.
