## Notes

- **Disclosed cash total:** $91,000 per the official 2019-2020 NVC Report PDF. Baby Box's combined cash from the 2020 NVC was $25,000 ($15,000 Grand Prize plus $10,000 Consumer Products 1st).
- Baby Box was the first Grand Prize winner from the Master of Innovation & Entrepreneurship (MIE) program.

## By the numbers (per 2019-2020 NVC Report)

- 114 Concept Paper submissions
- 43 teams advanced to semi-finals
- 6 teams advanced from the Wildcard round
- 10 finalist teams
- 5 Ant Tank judges
- 900+ virtual attendees at the Grand Finale
- 10 countries represented

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Baby Box** (Grand Prize): OPERATING and growing. Now **123 Baby Box**. Raised $1.2M oversubscribed in 2023; TechCrunch coverage notes 245% growth, tripled revenue and 5x MRR (2021 baseline) with a target of $100K MRR / $1M ARR. Launched 123 Book Box product line. Strongest non-medical operating company in the archive.
- **No Decaf Allowed** (Consumer Services 1st): ACQUIRED. Subscription absorbed into **Moongoat Coffee Roasters'** programme in December 2022 (asset transfer, not equity). The third documented exit in the archive after LAS to Medtronic and the Mechanodontics / BRIUS rebrand.
- **ImmunoTarget** (Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING (very early stage). Now **ImmunoTarget Therapeutics, Inc.**. Still active per a March 2025 bioRxiv preprint disclosure.
- **Bottle Built** (Audience Favorite): No public outcome found.
- **Conshus Clothing** (Social Enterprise 1st): No public outcome found.
