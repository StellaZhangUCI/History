## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: ~$100,000.
- This year used the *Grand Finale* / *Runner-Up* labeling rather than *Grand Prize* / *Runner-Up*; the meaning is the same.
- TeamMade was a UCI / Orange Coast College partnership entry; individual roster not published.
- **Cross-competition recognition (added 2026-05-12 per UCI Beall and Butterworth past-winners pages)**: Two 2023 NVC teams also won at sibling UCI competitions: **Leprendo** (NVC Runner-Up + CS 1st) had won the **Butterworth Product Development Competition 2022 1st Place** the year before the NVC win (with a 3-member subset: Nina Nguyen, Dylan Riffle, Dan Ta); **ChewRank** (NVC CS 2nd) placed **3rd in the Butterworth Product Development Competition 2023** the same year as the NVC appearance.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Leprendo** (Runner-Up + Consumer Services 1st): OPERATING. `leprendo.com` live with 2026 copyright and active product catalog. Reported $400K first-year sales. Pivoted from short-form-video framing to a more conventional online artisan-food marketplace.
- **Hyperion Therapeutics** (Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING. `hyperioneye.com` active; Newport Beach, CA; seed-stage biopharmaceutical with SP3/SP4/SP5 small-molecule pipeline in late preclinical.
- **Omni Pet Club** (Consumer Products 2nd): OPERATING. Modular multi-pet leash system selling direct at `omnipetclub.com`. (Earlier draft claimed retail distribution via Chewy / Walmart / Amazon; those listings are for a different brand, **OmniPet** by Leather Brothers, not the UCI startup.)
- **TeamMade** (Social Enterprise 2nd): OPERATING (non-commercial). UCI / Orange Coast College student team's **LuminOCity** modular ADU unit was donated to Homeless Intervention Services-OC and opened to house at-risk youth in July 2025.
- **VagAligna** (Grand Finale Winner): No public outcome found. Founder Fiza Qureshi has moved on professionally; project requires clinical trials and FDA approval.
- **Shower Power** (Social Enterprise 1st): No public outcome found.
