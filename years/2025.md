## Notes

- Disclosed cash total: $103,500 (sum of the cash-prize column above). Merage's announced pool was "$150,000+ in cash and in-kind"; the in-kind split is not itemized in indexed coverage.
- Several team rosters remain `Not Listed`. The official 2025 Beall Center NVC report PDF is the canonical source but is currently password-protected; see [`Sources & Notes`](../README.md#sources--notes) in the main archive.

## Post-competition outcomes (selected)

- **Grad2Go** (Grand Prize): OPERATING (brand new). Won May 22, 2025; too recent for outcome data. Confirmed competing again as a 2026 semi-finalist.
- **GlowGuard** (Runner-Up + Consumer Products 1st): OPERATING (brand new). Too recent for outcome data.
- **NasoCoustics** (Life Sciences 1st): OPERATING (design stage). `nasocoustics.uci.design` up; pre-incorporation.
- **PCOSitive Buddy** (MIE Award + Consumer Services 1st): OPERATING (likely). Founder Ricianne "Chi" Rey was hired in October 2025 as the UCI ANTrepreneur Center Program Lead and continues to be described as a PCOS-platform co-founder.
- **ServEasy** (Business Products 2nd): OPERATING (likely, finalist stage). Confirmed as a 2025 Stella Zhang finalist; no independent product traction yet.
- **Oslo / Birdhouse by Oslo** (Audience Favorite + Life Sciences 2nd): OPERATING (likely, finalist stage). Passive thermal-imaging vitals monitor for seniors; confirmed finalist.
