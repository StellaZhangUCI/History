## Priority 1: Time-sensitive gaps

### 2026 Grand Finale results

The 2026 Grand Finale is scheduled for May 21, 2026 (9 days from this review). After it concludes, the archive needs:

- Grand Prize, Runner-Up, Audience Favorite, MIE Award, and any other specialty awards
- Track 1st / 2nd winners for all 5 standard tracks (Business Products & Services, Consumer Products, Consumer Services, Life Sciences, Social Enterprise)

Likely sources: `merage.uci.edu/news/`, `beallinnovation.uci.edu`, OC Register, Business Wire press releases (typically published 1 to 3 days after the finale).

### Password-protected NVC annual reports (2021 onward)

The Beall Center publishes a comprehensive end-of-year PDF report for each NVC edition (2021 forward) at `merage.uci.edu/_files/documents/beall-center/nvc/` and `merage.uci.edu/_files/images/centers/innovation-entrepreneurship/`. **All are password-protected.** Unlocking access would fill most of the "Not Listed" team rosters in 2021 through 2024 in a single pass.

Suggested path: contact Sara Heimann (sheimann@uci.edu) at the Beall Center.

## How to claim a backlog item

1. (Optional) Open a GitHub issue with the item title (e.g., "Fill 2024 BagIt team roster") and comment with sources you've found.
2. Open a PR against the relevant files. For a roster fill, that's at minimum:
   - The team's row in `years/YYYY.md`
   - The matching award entry in `data/nvc.json`
   - (If applicable) the Cross-Year Analytics in `README.md`
3. Cite sources in the PR description.

You can skip step 1 and open the PR directly if the data is ready. See [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md) for full guidelines, source-quality preferences, and style notes.
